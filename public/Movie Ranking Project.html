My Top 3 Must-See Favorite Movies

Here's a list of the top 3 movies you must-see.

The 6th Man

A comedy, drama, fantasy, and sports film that is funny, entertaining, and heartwarming. One of my favorite sports movies of all time.

Mortal Kombat (1995)

An action pack martial arts film based on the popular fighting video game series. This is by far one of the best video game to movie adaptations ever made.

Trigun: Badlands Rumble

A Japanese anime film based on the Trigun manga series. A really fun, hilarious action movie. Highly recommend the "Trigun" anime series.
